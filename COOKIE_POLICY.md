# Pitchey Cookie Policy

## 1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how Pitchey uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our platform. This policy should be read alongside our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

Effective Date: December 3, 2025
Last Updated: December 3, 2025

## 2. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us recognize you, remember your preferences, and provide a better user experience.

## 3. Types of Cookies We Use

### Essential Cookies
**Purpose**: Required for basic platform functionality
**Legal Basis**: Legitimate interest (no consent required)
**Examples**:
- Authentication tokens
- Session management
- Security features
- Load balancing

### Performance Cookies
**Purpose**: Analyze platform usage and performance
**Legal Basis**: Consent required
**Examples**:
- Page load times
- Error tracking
- User interaction patterns
- Feature usage analytics

### Functional Cookies
**Purpose**: Enhance user experience and platform features
**Legal Basis**: Consent required
**Examples**:
- Language preferences
- Theme selections
- Dashboard customizations
- Saved searches

### Marketing Cookies
**Purpose**: Deliver relevant advertising and content
**Legal Basis**: Explicit consent required
**Examples**:
- Targeted advertisements
- Social media integration
- Email campaign tracking
- Conversion measurement
